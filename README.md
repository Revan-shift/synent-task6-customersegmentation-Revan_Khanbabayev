# Synent Technologies - Data Science Internship
## Task 6: Customer Segmentation using K-Means Clustering

### 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on **Market Basket Analysis** by grouping customers of a retail mall based on their spending habits and annual income. Using the **K-Means Clustering** algorithm, I identified distinct customer segments to help the business understand its target audience and tailor marketing strategies.

### 📊 Dataset Information
The **Mall Customers Dataset** contains the following features:
* **CustomerID:** Unique ID assigned to the customer.
* **Gender:** Gender of the customer.
* **Age:** Age of the customer.
* **Annual Income (k$):** Annual income of the customer.
* **Spending Score (1-100):** Score assigned by the mall based on customer behavior and spending nature.

### 🛠️ Technologies Used
* **Python** (Programming Language)
* **Scikit-learn** (KMeans Clustering Algorithm)
* **Pandas & Numpy** (Data Processing & Manipulation)
* **Matplotlib & Seaborn** (Data Visualization)

### 🚀 Implementation Steps
1.  **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Analyzed the distributions of income and spending scores.
2.  **The Elbow Method:** Implemented the Elbow Method to find the optimal number of clusters (Determined K=5).
3.  **Model Training:** Applied K-Means clustering to the dataset using the optimal K value.
4.  **Clustering Visualization:** Created a professional scatter plot to visualize the 5 distinct customer segments and their centroids.

### 💡 Key Insights (Customer Segments)
Through clustering, I identified 5 major groups:
* **Target Group:** High income and high spending score.
* **Careful:** High income but low spending score.
* **Sensible:** Low income and low spending score.
* **Spendthrifts:** Low income but high spending score.
* **Standard:** Average income and average spending.
